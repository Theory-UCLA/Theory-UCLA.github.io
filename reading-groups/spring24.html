About

Protocols for secure multiparty computation (MPC) allow a set of parties to compute a joint function of their private inputs while revealing nothing but the output. The potential applications for MPC are huge: privacy-preserving auctions, private DNA comparisons, private machine learning, threshold cryptography, and more. Recently, MPC has become efficient enough to be used in practice, and has made the transition from theory to industry.
